Detection Capabilities

🔗

Suspicious URL Detection

Identifies obfuscated, shortened, or deceptive URLs embedded in email content.

⚠️

Urgency Language Analysis

Flags manipulative phrases like "act now", "verify immediately", or "account suspended".

🧩

Domain Spoofing Check

Detects lookalike domains and homograph attacks designed to impersonate real brands.

📎

Attachment Risk Signals

Recognizes references to dangerous attachment types commonly used in malware delivery.

🏦

Brand Impersonation

Cross-checks for unauthorized use of brand names from banks, tech companies, and government agencies.

🔐

Credential Harvesting Signals

Detects prompts asking for passwords, PINs, SSNs, or other sensitive credentials.

Built for Awareness

PhishGuard was built as part of a Cybersecurity Summer Internship project to raise awareness about email phishing — one of the most common attack vectors in modern cybercrime.

Phishing attacks account for over 36% of data breaches globally. This tool empowers users to verify suspicious emails before clicking links or sharing sensitive information.
